## FAQ 5 real Q&As

**Q: Is this actually free?**
Yes, completely. No credit card, no trial period, no freemium limit.
The tool is free because it's built by Credex we make money if you
decide to buy discounted AI credits, not from charging you to audit.

**Q: How accurate are the savings estimates?**
Every number in the audit traces back to official vendor pricing pages,
verified weekly. The recommendations are based on plan-fit logic
team size, use case, and seat count not guesswork. We cite sources
for every pricing figure in our PRICING_DATA documentation.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
Your audit results are saved so you can share them via a unique URL.
The public version of your audit never includes your email or company
name only tool names and savings numbers. Your email is optional and
only collected after you've seen your results.

**Q: What AI tools does it cover?**
Currently: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API,
OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf. We update pricing weekly and add
new tools based on what teams are actually using.

**Q: What is Credex?**
Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits Cursor, Claude,
ChatGPT Enterprise, and API credits sourced from companies that
overforecast or pivoted. The discount is real: same API, same models,
lower price. AIAudit is a free tool we built to help teams understand
their spend before deciding whether Credex makes sense for them.
